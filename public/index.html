Jules BERRY

PhD student in Applied Mathematics

INSA Rennes

I am a second year PhD student in the Numerical Analysis team at IRMAR. I work on problems related to mean field games and partial differential equations on networks under the supervision of Olivier Ley and Francisco Silva. I am part of the ANR COSS project.
